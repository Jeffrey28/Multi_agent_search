Chang Liu (S’15) received the B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering and in AppliedMathematics (double major) in 2011 from Peking University, China. He received the M.S. degree in Mechanical Engineering and in Computer Science in 2014 and 2016 from the University of California, Berkeley, USA. He is currently working towards the Ph.D. degree in Mechanical Engineering. He is a Graduate Student Researcher with the Vehicle Dynamics and Control Laboratory, University of California, Berkeley, headed by Prof. J. Karl Hedrick and Prof. Francesco Borrelli. His research interests include robot path planning, distributed filtering, and human–robot collaboration.
